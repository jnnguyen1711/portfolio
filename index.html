Maps

Often passengers do not watch their steps and often happens too frequently that when they start to see, things are already dead. The series is conducted on my last day on college campus, along the roads I walked and ran during my young adults. This is to dedicate to all people who we once were and memories we could never experience again.

Speaking about fire and how it will burn me and fuel me and burn me again into beautiful ash, melting in the air. Burn me bright, and I will volunteer to die within the heart of this universe.
